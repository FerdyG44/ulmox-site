ULMOX Safety & Moderation

ULMOX is committed to creating a safe and respectful environment for all users.

Content Moderation

All content shared on ULMOX may be reviewed through automated detection systems and moderation tools.

We actively monitor and remove content that violates our policies, including:

  • Harassment or bullying
  • Hate speech
  • Sexual or explicit content
  • Violent or graphic material
  • Spam or misleading content
  • Illegal activity
  • Child exploitation or abusive behavior

Reporting System

Users can report any content directly inside the app.

Reported content is reviewed and may be:

  • Removed
  • Hidden from public feeds
  • Escalated for further review
  • Used to suspend or ban accounts

Blocking Features

Users can block other users at any time.

Blocked users can no longer interact with or appear in the reporting user’s experience.

AI-Assisted Detection

ULMOX may use automated systems to detect potentially unsafe or inappropriate content before it reaches wider distribution.

Content flagged by automated systems may undergo additional review.

User Safety

We encourage respectful communication and positive interactions.

Accounts that repeatedly violate our rules may be suspended or permanently banned.

Child Safety

ULMOX has zero tolerance for child sexual abuse material (CSAM) or exploitative behavior.

Any detected CSAM-related content or activity will be removed immediately and may be reported to relevant authorities.
